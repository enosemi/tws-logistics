About TWS Logistics

TWS Logistics Shipping Company (RSC) is a global logistics company that specializes in providing international logistics and courier services and solutions that enable its customers & partners the flexibility that today’s shippers require. Over the years, TWS Logistics Shipping Company (RSC) has been an industry leader in the delivery services to Financial Institutions, Publishers, Fashion Houses, Tech Firms, Government Agencies, Business Owners and lot more. Whatever you need to transport, whenever, wherever, however it needs to get there on-time and on-budget, TWS Logistics Shipping Company (RSC) makes it happen. In fact, since our founding, other major delivery services have used us to take care of their own deliveries.

Now, TWS Logistics Shipping Company (RSC)'s vast expertise and custom solutions are at your service. We are one of the leading service providers offering logistics and express courier services to corporate clients across the globe. Based on our ethical practices, we also provide ocean cargo clearance and export documentation services. Reckoned for our transparency and reliability, we provide logistics solutions via different channels such as ocean, air and land.
